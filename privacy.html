Your Data, Your Trust

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you subscribe to StreamVault, we collect the following information:

  • Name — to identify your account
  • Phone number — to communicate with you via WhatsApp
  • Subscription details — the service type, duration, and dates

We do not collect payment card details directly. Payments are handled through third-party services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Deliver and manage your streaming subscriptions
  • Send renewal reminders before your subscription expires
  • Respond to your questions and support requests
  • Improve our services and customer experience

3. How We Protect Your Information

Your data is stored securely and access is limited to authorized personnel only. We use industry-standard measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following cases:

  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights or the safety of our users
  • With service providers who help us operate (e.g., messaging platforms), under strict confidentiality

5. WhatsApp Communication

We use WhatsApp to communicate with you about your subscription, including account delivery and renewal reminders. By subscribing to StreamVault, you consent to receiving these messages. You may opt out at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via WhatsApp or through our contact page.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, reach out to us:

  • WhatsApp
  • Contact page
